## Getting Started

### Receiving Your Account

Your school will provide you with LibreCampus login credentials. You'll receive:
- An **email address** registered with the school
- A **temporary password**

### Logging In

1. Open your browser and navigate to your school's LibreCampus URL
2. Enter your email and password
3. On first login, you'll be prompted to change your password
4. Optionally enable two-factor authentication for added security

---

## Dashboard

After logging in, your dashboard shows a summary for your linked student(s):

- **Current GPA / Average Grade**: Overall academic performance
- **Attendance Rate**: Percentage of classes attended
- **Recent Grades**: Latest graded assignments
- **Upcoming Assignments**: Due dates for pending work

## Viewing Grades

### Gradebook Access

1. Navigate to **Gradebook**
2. Select your child's classroom from the dropdown
3. View grades organized by assignment category:
   - **Homework** scores
   - **Quiz** scores
   - **Exam** scores
   - **Project** scores
4. The final column shows the overall **letter grade**

### Understanding Grades

| Grade | Percentage | Meaning |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| **A** | 90–100% | Excellent |
| **B** | 80–89% | Good |
| **C** | 70–79% | Satisfactory |
| **D** | 60–69% | Needs Improvement |
| **F** | Below 60% | Failing |

Grades are calculated as weighted averages. Different assignment types may carry different weights (e.g., exams may count more than homework).

### Individual Assignments

Click on any classroom to see individual assignment scores with:
- Assignment title and type
- Maximum possible score
- Your child's score
- Teacher feedback (if provided)
- Submission date

## Attendance Records

### Viewing Attendance

1. Navigate to **Attendance**
2. View attendance history organized by classroom
3. Each record shows:
   - **Date** of the class
   - **Status**: Present, Absent, Late, or Excused

### Attendance Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **PRESENT** | Attended the class |
| **ABSENT** | Did not attend (unexcused) |
| **LATE** | Arrived late to class |
| **EXCUSED** | Absent with approved excuse |

If you believe an attendance record is incorrect, contact the teacher directly via messaging.

## Assignments

### Viewing Assignments

1. Navigate to **Assignments**
2. See all assignments across your child's classrooms
3. Each assignment shows:
   - Title and description
   - Classroom name
   - Due date
   - Status (submitted/pending/graded)

### Assignment Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **PUBLISHED** | Assignment is available; awaiting submission |
| **Submitted** | Your child has submitted their work |
| **Graded** | Teacher has reviewed and scored the work |
| **CLOSED** | Submission period has ended |

## Announcements

### Staying Informed

Navigate to **Announcements** to see school-wide and role-targeted announcements:

- **Pinned** announcements appear at the top — these are important
- Announcements are sorted by date (newest first)
- Some announcements may be targeted specifically to guardians

Check announcements regularly for:
- School events and holidays
- Policy changes
- Important deadlines
- General communications from administration

## Messaging Teachers

### Sending a Message

1. Navigate to **Messages**
2. Click **Compose**
3. Search for the teacher by name or email
4. Enter a subject and your message
5. Click **Send**

### Reading Replies

- Check your **Inbox** for new messages
- Unread messages show a badge indicator
- Click a message to read the full conversation

### Best Practices

- Use messaging for questions about your child's progress
- Include your child's name in the subject for clarity
- Be specific about the classroom or assignment you're asking about
- Allow 1-2 business days for a response

## Notifications

### Staying Updated

The bell icon in the top-right corner shows your notification count.

You'll receive notifications for:
- New grades posted
- Assignment deadlines approaching
- New announcements
- Messages from teachers

### Managing Notifications

1. Click the bell icon to see all notifications
2. Click **Mark as Read** on individual notifications
3. Use **Mark All as Read** to clear the badge

## Account Settings

### Changing Your Password

1. Navigate to **Settings**
2. Under **Change Password**:
   - Enter your current password
   - Enter a new password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Confirm the new password
3. Click **Update Password**

### Enabling Two-Factor Authentication (MFA)

For extra account security:

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Click **Enable MFA**
3. Install an authenticator app on your phone:
   - **Google Authenticator** (Android/iOS)
   - **Authy** (Android/iOS)
   - **Microsoft Authenticator** (Android/iOS)
4. Scan the QR code displayed on screen
5. Enter the 6-digit code from your app to verify
6. MFA is now active — you'll need the code each time you log in

### Profile Information

Your profile shows:
- Name and email address
- Assigned role (Guardian)
- Linked campus

Contact your school administrator to update personal information.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I link my account to my child?**
A: Your school administrator sets up the parent-student link. Contact the school office if your child doesn't appear in your dashboard.

**Q: Can I see grades in real-time?**
A: Grades appear as soon as the teacher submits them. You'll receive a notification when new grades are posted.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Contact your school administrator to reset your password.

**Q: Can I access LibreCampus on my phone?**
A: Yes! LibreCampus is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. No app installation is needed.

**Q: Who can see my messages?**
A: Messages are private between you and the recipient. Only campus administrators can access messages for administrative purposes.

**Q: How far back can I see grades and attendance?**
A: All records from the current and previous academic years are available.
